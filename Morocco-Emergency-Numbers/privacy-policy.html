Privacy Policy

Morocco Emergency Numbers — Last updated: February 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Morocco Emergency Numbers ("the App", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, available on iOS and Android.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

2. About the App

Morocco Emergency Numbers is an offline reference app that provides tourists and travelers in Morocco with quick access to emergency phone numbers, safety guides, embassy contacts, and related information in multiple languages.

The App is designed to work completely offline. All emergency data, guides, and embassy information are bundled within the App and do not require an internet connection.

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details.

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your selected language preference
  • Your selected display theme (light or dark mode)

These preferences are saved using your device's local storage and never leave your device. They can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the App.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, process, or share any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Camera, microphone, or contacts data
  • Any sensitive or financial information

5. Internet Access

The App requests internet access permission to enable optional features such as opening external links (e.g., embassy websites or legal pages) in your device's browser. No data is transmitted by the App itself during these interactions — your browser handles external links independently.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection SDKs. No data is shared with any third party.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). As the App collects no personal data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages.

8. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data at risk. Preferences stored locally on your device are protected by your device's own security mechanisms.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR or CCPA). Since we collect no personal data, there is no personal information held by us to access, correct, or delete.

If you wish to remove locally stored preferences, simply uninstall the App from your device.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
