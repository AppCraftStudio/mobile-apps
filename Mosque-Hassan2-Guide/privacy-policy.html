1. Introduction

Welcome to Mosque Hassan 2 Guide ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We do not require account registration. The App does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent in the App, and crash reports.

2.3 Location Information

The App may request access to your device's location solely to provide navigation and directions features (e.g., showing your position relative to the Mosque on the map). We do not store, share, or transmit your location data to any third party beyond what is required to display map functionality. Location access is entirely optional and the App remains functional without it.

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)

AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Users in applicable regions (EEA, UK) will be shown a consent dialog before personalized ads are displayed, in compliance with GDPR.

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
