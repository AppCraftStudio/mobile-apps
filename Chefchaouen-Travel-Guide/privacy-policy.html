Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Welcome to Chefchaouen Guide ("the App", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application available on iOS and Android.

Short version: Chefchaouen Guide is an offline-first travel guide. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on our servers. All your preferences are saved locally on your device only.

1 Information We Collect

Data Minimization

Chefchaouen Guide is designed with privacy by default. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device using local storage:

  • Your selected language preference
  • Your selected color theme (light or dark mode)
  • Content items you have bookmarked or saved

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

2 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or process any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location or GPS data
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, GAID, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to our servers
  • Payment or financial information

3 How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, we have no personal information to use, share, sell, or disclose. The locally stored preferences are used solely to:

  • Remember your preferred display language across sessions
  • Preserve your light or dark mode selection
  • Restore your saved or bookmarked content items

4 Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs. We do not use services such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any similar platform that would collect data about your behavior.

The App may include links to external websites (such as our Privacy Policy page or local business websites). Once you leave the App and visit an external site, that site's own privacy policy applies and we have no control over their data practices.

5 Children's Privacy

Chefchaouen Guide is a general-audience travel guide suitable for all ages. The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no personal data from any user, the App is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

If you believe a child has somehow submitted personal information through the App, please contact us at the address below and we will address the matter promptly.

6 Data Retention and Deletion

All data stored by the App (language preference, theme, bookmarks) resides locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Clearing the App's data through your device's application settings
  • Uninstalling the App from your device

We do not retain any copies of your data on external servers, so no additional deletion request to us is necessary.

7 Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights related to your personal data under laws such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), or other applicable regulations.

Since we do not collect, process, or store any personal data on our systems, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, export, or delete. All locally stored preferences can be cleared directly on your device as described in Section 6.

If you have questions about your privacy rights or wish to make a request, please contact us using the information below.

8 Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Because all App data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We encourage you to keep your device software up to date and to use a secure screen lock.

We do not transmit any data over the internet from the App, which eliminates the risk of data interception during transmission.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable laws. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.
