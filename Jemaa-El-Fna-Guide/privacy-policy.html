1. Introduction

Welcome to Jemaa El Fna Guide ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play).

By downloading and using Jemaa El Fna Guide, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the app.

2. About the App

Jemaa El Fna Guide is an informational travel guide app designed to help visitors explore and understand Jemaa El Fna square in Marrakech, Morocco. The app provides information about food, culture, performers, visit planning, and photography guidance.

Jemaa El Fna Guide does not require account registration, login, or the submission of any personal information to use the app's core features.

3. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We do not collect any personal information directly from you. The app does not include any forms, registration screens, or data submission mechanisms.

Information Collected Automatically

When you use the app, certain technical information may be collected automatically by the platform or third-party services embedded in the app, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics and crash reports (collected anonymously)
  • General location data (country/region level, for ad targeting purposes)
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) used by advertising networks

Device Permissions

The app may request access to the following device features:

  • Location: To provide navigation directions to Jemaa El Fna square. Location data is used solely on-device and is not stored or transmitted to our servers.
  • Internet access: Required to display advertisements and open external links.

You may deny these permissions at any time through your device settings. Denying location access will only disable navigation features; all other content remains fully accessible.

The information collected (primarily through third-party services) may be used to:
